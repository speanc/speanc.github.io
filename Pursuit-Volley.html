Pursuit Volley Summer Rebrand

Project Description

Rebranding Pursuit Volley to achieve a more “Synthwave” aesthetic, reinvigorating the brand for it’s Summer volleyball camps.

Reflection

I worked on this project jointly with my business partner in ‘Proto’, Eric Reger (Any collaborative projects labelled). We completed multiple jobs for Pursuit Volley over the course of summer 2020, encompassing social media posts, advertisements, and merchandise design. Working with the team at Pursuit Volley gave me the opportunity to stretch my legs with new styles and understand the importance of a healthy client-designer relationship.
