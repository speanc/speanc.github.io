About the Guy

Who's this bitch

Spencer Nelson has a variety of skills, and finds a lot of joy in finishing his work on time, while ensuring that it is of the highest quality

He has had an interest in graphic design since getting his hands on ‘Kid Pix’ in elementary, and ever since then has been polishing his skills in industry standard software. His main skills include, in no particular order, graphic design, 3D modeling and texturing, motion graphics, and video editing.

While attending the Lethbridge College Multimedia Production (MMP) program, he has moved into the freelance graphic design sector, working both solo and in a team based environment, and ensuring that clients were satisfied in every situation.

Sound like a good fit? Feel free to reach out!

Proto Graphic Design

Proto Graphic Design was the summer project of Spencer and Eric. Proto operated as a small graphic design business offering logo creation, social media content, and general art posted for enjoyment by public

Eric Reger

Eric is one of Spencer's closest collaborators, and has worked with him on numerous projects through Multimedia Production at Lethbridge College, as well as creating and working at Proto Graphic Design.
