Luminary Massage Therapy Various Branding

Objective

Provide the client with a logo designed with their specifications and needs in mind

Reflection

This project was the first official job as ‘Proto’ with Eric Reger (hyperlink to his site). We were approached to create a logo and brand identity for ‘Luminary Massage,’ a business located in Calgary, Alberta. We did a lot of experimentation and concepting, until landing on the final lotus design. Since then, we have been contacted to create variations of the logo for various reasons, as well as create new branding for an associated podcast ‘Holistic Journeys.’
